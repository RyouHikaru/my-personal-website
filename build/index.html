About

Hi, I am Carl James Manuel Reblora, My friends and colleagues call me CJ.

I'm currently working as a Software Engineer and consistently trying to learn and be updated in the modern technology.

I do a bunch of stuff like programming, gaming, and drawing. I listen to music a lot, if not all the time. I appreciate things, be it small or big.

Background

Born in the Gen Z era at Metro Manila, Philippines, young me have experienced both the traditional way and the dawn of digitalization.

I lived at different places my whole life, be it an urban or rural area. Adapted to different cultures and environment, that I guess, what makes me unique.
